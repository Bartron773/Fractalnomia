# Fractalnomia — Cartographies of Becoming

> **Can we recognize and steward the conditions that make the best human moments more likely?**

Fractalnomia investigates how attention, relationship, memory, and environment interact to shape *becoming* — at every scale.

Not prediction. Not optimization. **Stewardship.**

---

## Core Axioms

- We become through repetition.
- We flourish through relationship.
- We remain human through practice.
- Stewardship matters more than optimization.
